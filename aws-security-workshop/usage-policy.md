# Lab Environment Usage Policy

## Overview

This lab environment is provided for **training and educational purposes only**. By launching and using this environment, you agree to follow the usage rules outlined below.

---

## Acceptable Use

Users are allowed to:

* Use the lab environment to complete the provided training exercises.
* Configure resources and tools required for the workshop activities.
* Experiment with the provided services within the scope of the lab instructions.

---

## Prohibited Activities

Users must **not**:

* Perform illegal, harmful, or unauthorized activities.
* Attempt to access other users’ lab environments or accounts.
* Install unauthorized or malicious software.
* Use the lab resources for cryptocurrency mining or unrelated workloads.
* Attempt to bypass security controls or system restrictions.

---

## Resource Usage

* The lab environment resources are **temporary and limited**.
* All environments may be **automatically reset, suspended, or terminated** after the lab session or workshop period.
* Any data stored in the environment may be **deleted without notice**.

---

## Security and Credentials

* Do not share your login credentials with anyone.
* Do not expose sensitive information or keys in public repositories.
* Participants are responsible for protecting their account access during the lab session.

---

## Monitoring

For security and operational purposes, usage of the lab environment may be **monitored and logged**.

---

## Agreement

By launching the lab environment, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to follow this usage policy.

Failure to comply with these rules may result in **termination of your lab access**.
